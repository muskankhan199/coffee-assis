ARABICA

Coffea arabica, also known as the Arabic coffee, is a species of flowering plant in the coffee and madder family Rubiaceae.

ROBUSTA

Robusta coffee is notoriously bitter and is used primarily in instant coffee, espresso, and as a filler in certain blends of ground coffee.

EXCELSA

The taste of Excelsa coffee shares characteristics with both high and low-altitude coffees. It has a sweet, fruity, tart quality but is strong and dark.

Liberica has a strong and unique flavor, with some people saying that you either “love or hate it, with no in between”.
